ABOUT

An Usual Love Story is a 2025 Indian Tamil-language coming-of-age romantic comedy film written and directed by Dhanush, who produced jointly it with Kasthuri Raja and Vijayalakshmi Kasthuri under Wunderbar Films and RK Productions. The film stars Pavish Narayan and Mathew Thomas in the lead roles, with Anikha Surendran, Priya Prakash Varrier, R. Sarathkumar, Venkatesh Menon, Rabiya Khatoon and Ramya Ranganathan.
